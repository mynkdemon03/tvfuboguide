Common Fubo TV Login Issues and Solutions
------------------------------------------

Although the login process is generally smooth, users may occasionally encounter problems. Here are common issues and practical solutions.

Forgotten Password
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If you cannot remember your password:

- Click the “Forgot Password” link.  
- Enter your registered email address.  
- Follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.  

Incorrect Login Credentials
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Login failures often result from minor errors. Make sure:

- Caps Lock is turned off.  
- Your email address is typed correctly.  
- There are no extra spaces in the login fields.  

Fubo TV App Login Problems
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If the app is not allowing you to sign in:

- Restart your device.  
- Update the Fubo TV app to the latest version.  
- Check your internet connection.  
- Clear the app cache if supported.  
- Reinstall the application if needed.  

Internet Connection Issues
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

A stable internet connection is required for both login and streaming. Restart your router or switch to a more reliable network if necessary.
