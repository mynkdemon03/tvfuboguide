Common Fubo TV Login Problems and Fixes
----------------------------------------

Even though the login process is straightforward, users may occasionally face issues. Here are common Fubo TV login problems and how to resolve them.

Forgotten Password
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If you cannot remember your password:

- Select the “Forgot Password” option.  
- Enter your registered email address.  
- Follow the password reset instructions sent to your inbox.  

Incorrect Email or Password
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Login errors often occur due to small typing mistakes. Make sure:

- Caps Lock is turned off.  
- Your email address is spelled correctly.  
- There are no extra spaces in the login fields.  

Fubo TV App Not Working
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If the app is not allowing you to log in:

- Restart your device.  
- Update the Fubo TV app to the latest version.  
- Check your internet connection.  
- Clear the app cache if your device allows it.  
- Reinstall the app if necessary.  

Internet Connectivity Issues
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

A strong and stable internet connection is essential for logging in and streaming. Restart your router or switch to a more reliable network if needed.
